Week 1: Final Project Proposal

Project Idea 1: Fluid Wave Transport

- i've always thought it was cool how water can carry stuff to shore by itself

- i wanted to make a surface that moves things with waves instead of wheels

- when it's done it'll be a flat panel that ripples on its own

- you put a small object on one side and the wave carries it to the other

Image reference: Claude

Project Idea 2: Scout Bug

- i kept thinking about places that are too dangerous for people to go into

- like a collapsed building, where you still need to see inside

- so i wanted something tiny that can go in for us

- when it's done it'll be a little bug robot with a camera that sends video to my phone

Image reference: Claude

Project Idea 3: Power Glove

- grip is one of the first things people lose when they get old or hurt

- even opening a jar gets really hard

- i wanted to make something that gives that strength back

- when it's done it'll be a glove that feels you trying to grip and closes your fingers for you

Image reference: Claude
